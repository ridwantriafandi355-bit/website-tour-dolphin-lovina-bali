Your Full Day Itinerary

Premium Tour Schedule ($65 Package)

From pickup to drop-off — here's how your unforgettable day unfolds.

06:00 AM

Hotel Pickup

Our driver picks you up from your hotel in Ubud, Canggu, or Kuta. Sit back and enjoy the scenic drive to North Bali.

08:15 AM

Dolphin Swimming

Board your private boat at Lovina port and head out to swim alongside wild dolphins in the open Bali Sea.

09:30 AM

Coral Reef Snorkeling

Dive into crystal-clear waters and explore vibrant coral reefs teeming with tropical fish, just offshore from Lovina Beach.

10:30 AM

International Breakfast

Enjoy a freshly prepared international breakfast at a local restaurant by the beach. Fuel up for the rest of the adventure.

12:00 PM

Coffee Plantation Visit

Tour a traditional Balinese coffee plantation. Learn about Luwak coffee and sample a variety of local teas and coffees.

01:30 PM

Leke Leke Waterfall

Visit the stunning Leke Leke Waterfall hidden in the jungle. A short trek leads you to one of Bali's most photogenic waterfalls.

03:00 PM

Return to Hotel

Relax in your private transport as we drive you back to your hotel in Ubud, Canggu, or Kuta. What a day!
