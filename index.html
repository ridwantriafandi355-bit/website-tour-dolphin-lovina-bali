Dolphin Tour Schedule

Dolphin Tour Lovina — Your Sunrise Schedule

From the first light of dawn at Lovina Beach to a fulfilling breakfast by the shore in Singaraja, here's your complete dolphin tour itinerary.

06:00 AM
Sunrise Departure from Lovina Beach

Board your traditional outrigger boat at Lovina Beach, Singaraja and set sail into the golden sunrise. Hotel pickup available from Ubud, Canggu & Kuta.

08:30 AM
Dolphin Swimming & Snorkeling

Swim alongside wild dolphins in the open waters off North Bali. Then snorkel pristine coral reefs just minutes from Lovina Beach.

10:00 AM
Return & International Breakfast

Return to Lovina Beach refreshed. Premium guests enjoy an international breakfast and transport back to Ubud, Canggu, or Kuta.
